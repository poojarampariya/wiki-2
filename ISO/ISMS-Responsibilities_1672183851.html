Security Insident Response Team

End User

Initiating a security related incident/request through phone call, web interface and electronic mail to the Service desk.

Obtaining required approvals at business end before raising an operational request.

Providing necessary authentication information as requested by Service desk.

Reporting incidents that are encountered.

Reviewing and accepting the closure of call security related request or incident tickets

Service desk

Acknowledging and fulfilling assigned security related requests
Identify appropriate Security incident mgt support group

Perform required communication and notification to End Users

Logical Security Incident Mgt support group

Resolve the logical Incident and provide RCA. This is the IS team in WMSS

Depends on the Incident

Physical Security Incident Mgt Support group

Resolve the physical Incident and provide RCA (to be done by ISMS/Administration team)

Depends on the Incident
