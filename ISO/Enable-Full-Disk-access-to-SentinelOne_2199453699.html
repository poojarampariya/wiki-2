WM | IT SEC & ISO 27001 | All : Enable Full Disk access to SentinelOne

Created by Khushali Solanki, last modified on Apr 16, 2021

In order to work SentinelOne agent properly, you must Enable Full Disk access to SentinelOne as given below :

  1. Open System Preferences → Security and privacy → Privacy-> Full disk access → Give full disk access to all the below 3 components of SentinelOne

  2. If you do no see all or any of the 3 files mentioned above then please Click the + icon, press Command+Shift+G and navigate to:

/Library/Sentinel/sentinel-agent.bundle/Contents/MacOS/

or

Just Open Finder and click Go → Go to folder → Put the path “ /Library/Sentinel/sentinel-agent.bundle/Contents/MacOS/ ” → Go

and then drag the required files to the Full-disk access apps list to the right side

sentineld,

sentineld_helper.app

Sentinel_shell.app
