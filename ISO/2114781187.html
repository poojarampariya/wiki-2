WM | IT SEC & ISO 27001 | All : How to update/Reset keychain password in MAC

Created by Khushali Solanki on Mar 19, 2021

Update your keychain password

Updating your keychain password will require you to update passwords saved in the keychain as well.

  1. Open Keychain Access located in Applications > Utilities

  2. Select the Login keychain in the top left panel of the Keychain Access window.

  3. Select Edit > Change Password for keychain "Login." (Note: this option is grayed out if your login keychain password already matches your Mac login password.)

  4. Enter your *previous* password in the "Current Password" field, and your *current* WMSS password in the New Password and Verify fields. Click OK to save the changes.

Reset your keychain password

Resetting the keychain password will replace the existing keychain with a new, empty keychain.

  1. Open Keychain Access located in Applications > Utilities.

  2. Open the Keychain Access menu in the top left > Preferences... > Reset My Default Keychains.

  3. Type in your WMSS password.

  4. Click on the apple menu on the top left corner > Log out to reset the keychain and use it at login.
